Our Mission

CII uses a unique combination of legal expertise, forensic analysts and advanced technology to detect and take down pirated content in real time.

Designed for rights holders and broadcasters to combat piracy, CII powers companies to recapture views that would otherwise be lost to pirated streams – protecting IP and revenue.
